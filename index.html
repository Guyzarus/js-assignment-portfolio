About Me

Originally from Brooklyn, NY, I currently reside in sunny Orlando, FL, looking for a great environment to start planting my tech roots. My interest in software development originally began in simper days of Myspace when we were unknowingly utilizing HTML/CSS code to impress each other. I've always had a passion for computers, but life led me on a different path. In 2015, I graduated from the University of Central Florida and started a career in logistics sales.

It was a rollercoaster of highs and lows that I did quite well in, but it lacked the consistency I wanted for the future. So the heels of another tech boom, I enrolled at Seminole State College and graduated with my Bachelor's in Information Technology in 2021. I discovered the FastTrack program through a colleague who showed very promising results upon graduation and advised that I'd be a great candidate for the program, the rest is history.
